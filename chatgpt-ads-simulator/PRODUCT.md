# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mid-market DTC marketers, growth leads, and independent agency operators managing early ChatGPT Ads campaigns. They are trying to move from a rough campaign idea to a launch-ready structure without guessing whether context hints are too narrow, copy is weak, or budget will fail to spend.

## Product Purpose

ChatGPT Ads is the planning, simulation, launch, and optimization layer for OpenAI Ads Manager. It turns natural-language campaign intent into structured campaigns, predicts delivery before spend, prepares launch assets, and helps marketers compare predicted performance with real results after campaigns go live.

Success means a user can understand what to launch, why it should work, what to change before spending, and what the platform will do next.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, helpful. The product should feel like a competent media strategist sitting beside the user: clear about tradeoffs, transparent about uncertainty, and quick to turn analysis into practical actions.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI dashboard tropes: purple gradients, glass panels, oversized hero metrics, chatbot-first interfaces, decorative blobs, and vague "optimize everything" language. Avoid raw ads-manager austerity too; the interface should reduce anxiety, not feel like a spreadsheet with buttons.

## Design Principles

1. Show the full advertising object model: campaigns, ad groups, ads, context hints, creative assets, bids, and results should stay visibly connected.
2. Explain predictions through evidence: surface match rates, intent quality, confidence, and reasoning close to the recommendation.
3. Make AI feel inspectable: every generated draft should be editable, regeneratable, and validated inline.
4. Keep launch trust high: preflight checks, API actions, policy checks, and audit history should be obvious before anything is sent to OpenAI.
5. Favor workflow momentum: every screen should make the next useful action clear without hiding detail from power users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for all body text and controls. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, reduced motion, readable table layouts, and color-independent status labels so match quality is understandable without relying on red/amber/green alone.
